ABOUT US
freshly baked goodness,served with warmth.

founded in 2010 by Grace & amp; Samuel kariuki with a single oven and a dream to bring artisan baking to Nairobi.

Over the years we have grown into a beloved neighbourhood bakery trusted by thousands of families, offices and event planners across the city. Our bakers rise before dawn every single day to ensure that what lands on your table is always fresh, always flavourful and always made with care.

We belive baking is an art not a factory process. That is why we never use artificial preservatives, and we source our flour and dairy from certified farms within 100km of Nairobi
